Delete Your Account

How to delete your Hindsight account

  1. Open the Hindsight app
  2. Go to the Settings tab (gear icon)
  3. Scroll down to the Data section
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm by tapping Continue in the first dialog
  6. Type DELETE in the confirmation field
  7. Tap Delete Everything

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your account information (email, display name)
  • All decisions, reasoning, and expected outcomes
  • All outcome reflections and calibration data
  • Notification preferences and tokens
  • Your Firebase Authentication account

Important notes

  • This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • If you have an active Hindsight Pro subscription, please cancel it separately through the Google Play Store or Apple App Store before deleting your account to avoid future charges.

Need help?

If you're unable to delete your account through the app, contact us at thehindsightapp@gmail.com and we will process your request.
