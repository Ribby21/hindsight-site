Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 13, 2026

Hindsight ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal decision journal. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

When you use Hindsight, we collect:

  • Account information: Email address, display name, and authentication credentials (managed by Firebase Authentication).
  • Decision data: The decisions, reasoning, expected outcomes, moods, confidence levels, and outcome reflections you enter into the app.
  • Usage metadata: Timestamps, device type, and app interaction events (e.g., decisions created, outcomes logged) for analytics purposes.
  • Push notification tokens: Firebase Cloud Messaging tokens to deliver follow-up reminders you have opted into.
  • Subscription status: Managed by RevenueCat to determine your plan (Free or Pro).

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the core app functionality: storing, displaying, and analyzing your decisions.
  • To send follow-up reminders at intervals you have configured.
  • To calculate your calibration score and dashboard insights.
  • To manage your subscription status.
  • To improve the app through aggregated, anonymized analytics.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore) with security rules that restrict access to your own account. All data is encrypted in transit via HTTPS/TLS. Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, database, analytics, and push notifications. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all your decision data and personal information are permanently deleted from our servers.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access all your data within the app at any time.
  • Delete your account and all associated data from Settings > Delete Account.
  • Opt out of push notifications from Settings > Follow-up Reminders.

7. Children's Privacy

Hindsight is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at thehindsightapp@gmail.com.
